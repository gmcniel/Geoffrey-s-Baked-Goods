Welcome to Geoffrey's Baked Goods

Discover our delicious baked goods, made with love and the finest ingredients. From cookies to cakes, we have something for everyone.
Geoffrey has had a passion for baking since he was just a small child and would bake cookies, muffins, cakes and even brownies with his late grandma.
When it comes to baking, he sure does know what he is doing. Don't let his size fool you, his heart is big for ensuring that each baked good is made with passion and dedication to providing anyone with a top quality item.
As mentioned, he's been baking goods for over 20 years, ensuring that each item is made with the highest quality ingredients and attention to detail.
With a cottage bakery approach, Geoffrey ensures and makes completely sure that no burnt goods are ever included and no underbaked goods are ever given to the consumers.
